Battle Royal

is a free-to-play battle royale video game released on March 10, 2020, for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and Microsoft Windows. The game is a part of 2019's Call of Duty: Modern Warfare and 2020's Call of Duty: Black Ops: Cold War.

The game features three main modes: Plunder, Resurgence, and Battle Royale. Warzone introduces a new in-game currency system which can be used at "Buy Stations" in and around Verdansk.
